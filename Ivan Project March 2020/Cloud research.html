Hyperautomation

Hyperautomation allows organisations to automate and complete more complex work and solutions.
It deals with the application of advanced technologies such as machine learning and AI to increasingly automate processes and augment humans.
The role of machine learning is critical as it helps to increase the speed at which automation can take place, and to extend the limits of hyperautomation possibilities.
It enables the automation of processes that were once deemed the exclusive working rights of knowledge workers alone.
